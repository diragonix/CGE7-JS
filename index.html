Shortcuts:

press CTRL + Z to undo actions

press G to toggle the grid

pressing F enables fill mode,

holding CTRL while filling will fill every identical character

use ←↑→↓ to move the character selector

hold SHIFT to only draw the colors, not the character

press S to swap colors

press D to fill last stroke with current selection

press RIGHT CLICK to copy character
